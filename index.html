About Me

I'm a full-stack developer with four years of experience building and maintaining web applications on a federal government contract at ICF, supporting the Social Security Administration. My work has spanned PHP/Laravel backends, MySQL databases, and accessibility-compliant frontends — all within a high-compliance environment governed by FISMA and Section 508 standards.

My current focus is Python-based backend development: building REST APIs with FastAPI, designing ETL data pipelines, and integrating AI tooling using the Claude API. I'm drawn to work at the intersection of clean backend architecture and practical automation.

I'm actively pursuing roles in backend development, data engineering, and AI integration — particularly on teams building APIs, pipelines, or developer tooling. I hold an AWS Cloud Practitioner certification and a Master's in Information Technology.
